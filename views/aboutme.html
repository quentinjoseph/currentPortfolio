About Me

For eight and a half years I've been the touring drummer for singer/dj/recording artist Mayer Hawthorne. Although music has always been a huge passion of mine, the amount of time away from home was difficult. I decided it was time to pursue another passion, coding. I began the process of learning to code on my own using programs such as Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp to get my footing. As time moved on I felt as though I wanted to dig deeper so I enrolled in a full time intensive coding bootcamp to get a firm foundation. The Front End/JavaScript Bootcamp I enrolled in was a 2 month long full-time program that taught HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, NodeJS (and npm), PostgreSQL, and more. Since graduating I've been working as a web developer intern at Deep Canvas. I've learned an incredible amount there and I'm eager to continue pushing myself to grow and develope into an better web developer
